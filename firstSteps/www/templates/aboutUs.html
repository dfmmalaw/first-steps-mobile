Why First Steps?

We all want to remember our childrens' First Stepsand other milestones. I remember takiing so many photos, videos and writing so many entries in a journal to capture all of these majic moments. Then we would spend time uploading everthing to YouTube, Facebook, Dropbox etc..

I knew there had to be an easierier way and that is when I created First Steps. It is an app that is extremely easy to use and it will hopefully make as much of an impact on your life as it has on mine.

Thank you to all the parents out there that have made this possible...
